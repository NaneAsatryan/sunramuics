Welcome to Sunramics!

Studio where you can create personalized pottery and enjoy a cup of coffee. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced potter, Sunramics is for YOU!

Craft your own mug or plate. After two days of firing and glazing, pick up your creation and enjoy a complimentary coffee or snack served in your handmade piece

How it works?

1. Book Your Session: Select a perfect time for you during the day

2. Create Your Pottery: your unique mug, vase or plate

3. Leave your creation with us for two days for firing and glazing.

4. Pick Up & Enjoy: Collect your pottery and get a free coffee or snack served in/on your masterpiece.

(Or just buy coffee or sweets without touching any clay)
